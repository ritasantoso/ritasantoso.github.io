About Me

I am from College Station, Texas, and I'm currently pursuing a BFA at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. My interests besides making artwork include eating bread, eating, and bread.

The beauty of art for me is that it's a manmade creation, utilized to transition from using the mind to fulfill the body but instead using the different senses to stimulate the mind. I experiment with different techniques to create a unique sensation in each piece, resulting in a wide variety of styles throughout my portfolio. While I hope to find a one that I stick with one day, I enjoy discovering the way each style speaks to me. The longer you observe each piece, the more layers of detail unfold.
